The Loop

Every death feeds the next descent.

You will fall. ASHMOURN counts on it — and so should you. What you carry out of the dark becomes the strength that takes the next run deeper.

Darkness
The residue of every run. Spend it at the Court on permanent upgrades that persist past death.

Provoke the underworld to bite back harder. Steeper danger, richer spoils — the climb is yours to pace.
Blessings
Claim boons from the old powers still lingering below. Stack and chain them into a build that turns desperation into dominance.
